The problem: AI advances rapidly while governance lags in fragmented systems

• Redundant frameworks create confusion
• Ethical concepts get lost in translation
• Voluntary standards lack enforcement
• Geopolitical divides block coordination

---

Key challenges:

1) Translating fairness, transparency into actionable standards—these values are contested and context-dependent

2) Voluntary compliance enables cherry-picking for reputation over accountability

---

3) "15 competing standards" problem creates decision paralysis

4) Pacing mismatch: standards take years, AI risks emerge in months

5) Geopolitical fragmentation blocks global alignment

[Attach image: img_1.jpg]

---

🚀 Our solution: Human-centered, harmonized, adaptive governance

• Global collaboration using crosswalk tools
• Layered approach from frameworks to operational tools
• Agile governance with living documents
• Strengthened auditing ecosystems

Critical: Embed diverse stakeholder voices throughout

From marginalized communities to Global South perspectives—inclusive councils ensure AI works for everyone, not just tech elites
