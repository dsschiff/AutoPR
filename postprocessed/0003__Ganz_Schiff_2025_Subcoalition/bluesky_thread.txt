Organizations aren't monolithic. They're arenas of competing goals where teams form factions—subcoalitions—with aligned preferences.

Most models treat orgs as unified. Reality? These fault lines drive decision-making chaos.

---

The problem? Subcoalitions are hard to observe and model. Machine learning struggles with incomplete data and group dynamics.

SCA breakthrough: Maps hidden fault lines through preference-based clustering.

How it works:
✅ Seeds meaningful coalitions
✅ Fine-tunes preference alignment
✅ Stops when no one wants to defect

Result? Stable, representative subcoalitions that explain conflict patterns.
