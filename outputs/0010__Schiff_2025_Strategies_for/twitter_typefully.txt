🚨 AI governance is fragmented. Over 500 standards, overlapping frameworks, contradictory regulations create a maze for organizations.

But there's a roadmap to harmonize this and build human-centered AI governance 🧵

---

🤔 The core problem: AI advances at breakneck speed, but governance tools lag behind in fragmented systems.

Key challenges:
• Redundancy & overlap
• Decision paralysis 
• Ethical concepts lost in translation
• Geopolitical divides

---

Challenge 1: Translating fairness, transparency, well-being into actionable standards is HARD.

Why? These values are context-dependent and contested. Standards risk oversimplifying or "ethics-washing" - leaving structural inequities unaddressed.

---

Challenge 2: Voluntary standards dominate without enforcement.

Organizations cherry-pick compliance, creating superficial audits and reputational signaling instead of meaningful accountability.

---

Challenge 3: The "15 competing standards" problem.

Proliferation leads to decision paralysis. Organizations face patchwork frameworks without clear guidance on priorities or integration.

[Attach image: img_1.jpg]

---

Challenge 4: The pacing problem.

AI evolves faster than governance. Standards take years to develop, but AI risks emerge in months. Result? Obsolete standards that fail to keep up.

---

🚀 The solution: Human-centered, harmonized, adaptive AI governance.

Our roadmap starts with global collaboration among ISO, IEEE, OECD. Use crosswalks to map overlaps and reduce redundancy.

[Attach image: img_2.jpg]

---

Adopt a layered approach:
• High-level frameworks (NIST AI RMF)
• Domain-specific guidelines (IEEE 7010 for well-being)
• Operational tools (model cards)

This ensures scalability and adaptability.

---

Make governance agile:
✅ Living documents that evolve with AI tech
✅ Rapid-response taskforces for real-time updates  
✅ Regulatory sandboxes to test compliance frameworks

---

Strengthen auditing ecosystems:
• Train independent accreditation bodies
• Promote transparency in audit results
• Use practical tools like datasheets and checklists to bridge principles-practice gap

[Attach image: img_3.jpg]

---

Finally: Embed diverse stakeholder voices in every step.

From marginalized communities to Global South perspectives - inclusive councils ensure AI works for everyone. 🌍
