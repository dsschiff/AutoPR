The AI job market is evolving beyond coding. Employers now demand AI ethics and governance skills at unprecedented rates. Our analysis of 4M+ job postings from 2018-2023 reveals what's driving this shift 🧵

https://doi.org/10.1109/TTS.2025.3567143

[Attach image: img_1.jpg]

---

AI is transforming industries, but with great power comes great responsibility. We're seeing an "AI skills gap"—a shortage of professionals equipped with both technical expertise AND the ability to handle ethical dilemmas and regulatory challenges 📊

---

Our study analyzed 4.4M+ AI-related job postings to uncover trends in demand for AI ethics (fairness, transparency) and AI governance (regulatory compliance, risk management). Published in IEEE Transactions on Technology and Society 📈

---

Key finding: AI ethics ≠ AI governance. Employers seek distinct but complementary skills:

🔹 Ethics roles: Data privacy, bias mitigation, critical thinking
🔹 Governance roles: Risk management, policy development, leadership

Both require interdisciplinary knowledge

---

Demand is surging 🚀 AI ethics roles grew from 35K in 2018 to 109K in 2022. Governance roles hit 96K in 2022. Even as overall AI hiring dipped in 2023, these roles remained stable. Results suggest sustained market need

---

Not all sectors move at the same pace. Finance and Information industries dominate demand, with AI ethics/governance roles growing fastest there. Why? Highly regulated sectors can't afford ethical lapses 🏦

[Attach image: img_2.jpg]

---

What's driving this alignment? Governments introduce new AI regulations demanding compliance. Employers recognize public trust is critical for AI adoption. Universities race to create relevant programs 🎓

---

The opportunity: More than 100K professionals needed annually in these fields. Many organizations struggle to find talent. For those entering the field, this represents significant demand outpacing supply

---

AI ethics and governance aren't "nice-to-haves"—they're becoming non-negotiable pillars of responsible AI development. As industries adopt AI at scale, these roles will define how society benefits from this technology ⚖️
